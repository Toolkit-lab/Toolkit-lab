Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tools

Are these tools completely free to use?

Yes, all our tools are 100% free with no hidden costs, registration requirements, or limitations. We believe in providing valuable tools that are accessible to everyone.

How do you ensure the privacy of my files?

All file processing happens directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your computer and are not uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security for your sensitive documents.

What file formats do you support for conversion?

We support a wide range of formats including JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF for images, and PDF, DOCX, PPTX for documents. Our Image Converter and Document Converter tools support the most popular formats.

Is there a file size limit for processing?

Since processing happens in your browser, the limitations depend on your device's capabilities. Generally, modern browsers can handle files up to 2GB, but for optimal performance, we recommend files under 500MB.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation is required. Our tools work directly in your web browser. All you need is an internet connection to access our website, and the processing happens locally on your device.

How can I support ToolKit?

The best way to support us is by sharing our tools with others who might find them useful. You can also follow us on social media and provide feedback to help us improve our tools.

Our team of developers and designers are passionate about creating tools that solve real problems for our users. We believe that everyone should have access to powerful file processing tools without having to pay expensive subscription fees or install complicated software.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

At ToolKit, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website and tools.

Information We Don't Collect

The most important thing to know about ToolKit is that we process all files locally in your browser. This means:

  • Your files never leave your computer
  • We never upload your files to our servers
  • We don't have access to your files
  • We don't store your files

Information We Do Collect

We collect minimal information to help us improve our services:

  • Usage Analytics: We use anonymous analytics to understand how people use our tools so we can improve them
  • Error Reports: If a tool crashes, we may receive an anonymous error report to help us fix the issue
  • Basic Device Information: We collect information about your browser and device to ensure our tools work correctly

Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide analytics about how our tools are used
  • Ensure the proper functioning of our website

Third-Party Services

We use a few trusted third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: To understand how our website is used
  • Cloudflare: For content delivery and security

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@toolkit.com.
